Welcome to GARN kbh

We are a new store in the heart of Valby that sells yarn and knitting accessories for every taste and budget.

Our yarns are from well-known suppliers that the popular knitting designers like to use in their patterns.

With us you will also find exclusive hand-dyed yarn, silk and cashmere. And we carry the most delicious luxury knitting accessories from Seeknit, Cocoknits and MUUD.

We take pride in helping our customers, so do not hesitate if you need guidance or want to borrow our yarn winders.
